My First Post: Building This Portfolio

Published on June 15, 2025

Welcome to my new little corner of the internet! I'm excited to finally have a space to share my thoughts, projects, and whatever else comes to mind. For my very first post, it felt fitting to talk about the creation of this portfolio website itself.

This site was built using a modern tech stack that I'm really passionate about: React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. The entire process was streamlined with the help of an AI-powered editor called Lovable, which allowed me to focus on the design and functionality while it handled much of the boilerplate code. It was a fascinating experience watching the components come to life with just a few prompts.

I wanted the design to be clean, modern, and easy to navigate. The dark theme was chosen to be easy on the eyes, and the purple accent color adds a bit of personality. Every section, from the hero banner to the project cards, was carefully considered to reflect both functionality and aesthetic.
